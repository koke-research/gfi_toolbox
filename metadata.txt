description=Delineate flood-prone areas from a DEM using the Geomorphic Flood Index GFI = ln(hr/H), with a 2.0 hierarchical backwater (river-to-confluence) update.
about=Pure-Python (NumPy) implementation of the Geomorphic Flood Index toolbox (Samela et al. 2017; Manfreda & Samela 2019; Albertini et al. 2022). Computes a continuous channel network from a channel-initiation threshold (Giannoni 2005), Strahler stream order, hillslope-to-river and river-to-confluence topological mappings, GFI 1.0 and GFI 2.0, and ROC-based threshold calibration against a reference flood map (ROCcurve_maggiore method). Outputs include GFI rasters, binary flood maps, HAND, water depth, Marginal Area, and a CSV performance report (AUC, CSI, Kappa, F1, TPR, TNR, FPR, FNR, Bias). No external dependencies beyond QGIS / GDAL / NumPy.
